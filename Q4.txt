Describe the best project you have worked on, including links if any:

"RaNoLa NFT Marketplace - Frontend" was one of the best projects I did so far for which I got appreciations from one of the judges of Tezasia Hackathon 2021 and also from fellow developers. With just one click in this software, a user can login to his Metamask wallet. My design's primary goal is to support artists financially by allowing them to sell their creations on this marketplace's. I used HTML5, CSS3, React.js, Styled Components, Node.js, and Material UI for this project.

Preview link: https://ranola-nftmp.netlify.app/
GitHub Repository: https://github.com/SairamNomula/RaNoLa-NFT-Marketplace
